We Are Platform Engineers

We believe that software systems must age well to justify their original investment. What has traditionally been the aim of software architecture, can more effectively be achieved with a well designed platform. We engineer platforms that serve as the true backbone for any digitally enabled business strategy.

Especially large enterprises with a strong focus on processes can effectively use a platform approach to increase the adoption of the changes that usually come with digital transformation activities, especially when moving towards a product management organization with highly autonomous product teams.

Digital Business Strategy

Build a platform as a foundational layer of your digital business strategy. Establish and facilitate the IT organisational model and general architectural concerns through an implementing software system.

Especially when transitioning towards Cloud and Edge a well designed platform approach helps keep security and cost under control.

Application Development

Platforms jump-start your development teams by centralizing common but repetitive devops activities into a single software system. In addition, platforms can be designed to help development teams to automatically follow architectural convictions of the organisation without an endless series of architectural alignment meetings.

Infrastructure Management

Platforms streamline infrastructure automation. They anchor configurations in a central structural model, provide clear naming conventions or even implement common infrastructure automation stacks. With this approach you can realize what is best described as decentralized, coordinated infrastructure management.
